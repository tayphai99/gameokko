Sylvan Soul: [General, Mark] Triggers whenever the enemy launches an attack. Drains their ATK by 20% of their starting ATK (max 280% of his own starting ATK) and lowers their Move Bar by 15%. At the end of their turn, removes 2 stacks of the draining effect. Each mark can trigger up to 3 times. The draining effect can stack up to 3 times.
